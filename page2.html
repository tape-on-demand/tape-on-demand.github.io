Tape-On-Demand Distribution Capabilities

Benefits of on-demand manufacturing

The biggest advantage to on-demand manufacturing is distribution. On-demand manufacturing opens the door to the most seamless, lowest cost, friction-free distribution capabilities. It offers you the highest payouts for your product and is without question the path of least resistance to where you wish to go.


Moreover, on-demand manufacturing allows you to minimize inventory and have maximum flexibility. You can easily change your product, discontinue products, and create new products. This is what the 21st century is all about. 

Use Tape-On-Demand to drop-ship to customers, distributors, stores, retail, radio and more

You use your own methods to sell your product. You may sell your products on your own Amazon or eBay store. Your products may be sold on other retail and wholesale outlets. As you get orders, you can use our submission tool and drop ship one or more products directly to your customers or outlets. You will receive the tracking ids for each order. If you use BandCamp, we're a BandCamp Fulfillment partner, we can easily mark your orders as shipped without you even needing to input tracking numbers.

This drop-ship capability reduces your shipping expenses because the shipment goes directly to where you need it. And, of course you do not need to manufacture and store inventory. Example: Tape-On-Demand will manufacture ($6) and ship your product ($3) for $9 to any location in the USA

We also operate as a bulk duplication service.

If you prefer, we can manufacture orders, and ship them to you in bulk, and you can ship them yourself, we offer different colors, tape lengths and quantity discounts that way, so if you want to put in the work, feel free!
